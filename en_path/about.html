Company Introduction

Scalpel Medical Technology(Wuhan) Co.Ltd is a company devoted to the agent sales of the imported medical apparatus and instruments in the area of chronic diseases and the R&D of medical instruments. Recently our agent products nationwide include the puncture series from Italian company STERYLAB S.r.l, endogo® HD endoscope from American company Envisionier Medical Technologies Company and portable adjustable negative pressure drainage from Dutch company the Medical & Woundcare Company.

This year, Scalpel Medical Technology(Wuhan) Co.Ltd and Medscience-tech Technology(Wuhan) Co.Ltd have tightly united to set the goal of development in the area of chronic diseases by combining together the mature online medical information mobile service platform of MAIDE, national medical specialists clients resources accumulated in the area of chronic diseases as well as all the patients customers and SHIKANGPEI’s traditional and strong network of national dealers. We hope to bring benefit to Chinese doctors and patients of chronic diseases with more professional products and services.
